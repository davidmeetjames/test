Personal Loan

Whatever your borrowing needs a personal loan may be able to help you achieve your goals. Our Personal Loan calculator can help you work out what your monthly repayments could be, based on our representative rate for the amount you enter.



Why get a Personal Loan with us?

  • Borrow from £1,000 to £25,000 Spread your repayments over 1 to 5 years for loans of £15,000 or less, or over 1 to 8 years for loans over £15,000.
  • Get a quick decision As an MidFirst current account customer, you could get an instant credit decision. Non-MidFirst current account customers should be contacted with a credit decision in 2-5 working days.
  • Quick access to funds Existing MidFirst current account customers could receive the money instantly once the application has been approved and the loan agreement has been signed. Non-MidFirst current account customers could receive the money into a nominated account 3 working days after the signed loan agreement is received.
  • Top up your existing loan If you’ve already got an MidFirst Personal Loan, find out about topping up your existing loan.
  • Make overpayments free of charge This may reduce the amount of interest you pay.
